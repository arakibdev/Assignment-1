About Vercel

Vercel is a dynamic brand with a mission to empower developers in creating and launching exceptional, high-performing applications and websites. With a focus on innovation, Vercel offers an array of products and services designed to streamline the development process. Their platform boasts features like zero configuration, allowing developers to concentrate on their code while benefitting from cutting-edge infrastructure that ensures speed and reliability.


Vercel's Edge Functions enable the creation of dynamic pages that load with static speed, optimizing user experience. Real-time analytics offer valuable insights for peak performance. Vercel's storage solution provides serverless storage for the frontend, simplifying data management.


Additionally, their comprehensive documentation and extensive library of templates make development even more accessible. Supported by an impressive lineup of individual investors, Vercel combines expertise from tech pioneers and industry leaders. Join Vercel in shaping the future of the web and explore the exciting career opportunities they offer.


Find your place within Vercel's product and infrastructure divisions, or contribute to their open-source ecosystem. Experience the power of Vercel and elevate your development projects to new heights
